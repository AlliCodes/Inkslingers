Why is Inkslingers so badass? Glad you asked!

InkSlingers was established in 2019. The project was the brainchild of our fearless leader, Gwen Little. With her guidence, fellow coherts, Haleigh and Allison will join her in becoming published authors. This of course, will lead to the ultimate goal of taking over the world! One book at a time.

Gwen Little

Gwen has been writing, basically, since she could hold a pen.
As a child, she loved being read to, and as soo as she could read on her own, loved the library and all the portable magic it contained. She uses her unique talents to share her passions for homeschooling and creating love and generosity through her blog geezgwen.com

Haleigh Hall

While it may be true that this is no longer her legal name, she is and always will be little Haleigh Hall to us.
Haleigh love books, from being read to as a toddler, to devouring boks by the dozen as an adult, writing her own story is the natural next step.
Heleigh has a creative mind, and a inate sence of wonder that lead her to tell engaging tales.

Allison Bennett

Allison was born into a home full of books. She was read to throughout her childhood by both her mother, Pam, and big sister, Gwen until she was old enough to read to them. To which they graciously listened.
Allison has enjoyed writing as a hobby for many years, and thanks to the encouragment of her big sister, is embarking on the journey to be published.
